Overview

As Interdisciplinary Project Society (IPS), we show maximum sensitivity to the security of your personal data. With this awareness, we attach great importance to the processing and preservation of all kinds of personal data belonging to all persons associated with our community in accordance with the Law on the Protection of Personal Data No. 6698 (KVKK).

With full awareness of this responsibility, we process your personal data as "Data Controller" under the KVKK, as explained below and within the limits ordered by the legislation.

Purpose of Processing Personal Data

  • check_circle Execution and management of community membership processes.
  • check_circle Planning and announcing events, project and training organizations.
  • check_circle Ensuring communication with members and evaluating feedback.
  • check_circle Fulfillment of legal obligations (Associations Desk notifications etc.).
  • check_circle Ensuring the security of the website and digital platforms.

warning Important Note on Data Sharing

As a rule, your personal data is not shared with third parties. However, it may need to be shared with authorized public institutions and organizations (e.g. Ministry of Interior, Courts) due to legal obligations. In addition, anonymized statistical data may be used within the scope of event sponsorships.

Your Rights Under Article 11 of KVKK

Right to learn whether my data is processed expand_more
You have the right to request information about whether your personal data is processed, and if so, information about it.
Right to request deletion or destruction of my data expand_more
You can request the deletion or destruction of your personal data within the framework of the conditions stipulated in the KVKK.
Right to request correction of data expand_more
You have the right to request correction of your personal data if it is incomplete or incorrectly processed.
Right to object and claim compensation expand_more
You have the right to object to a result against you by analyzing the processed data exclusively through automated systems and to demand compensation for the damage in case you suffer damage due to unlawful processing.
