Smart Green Campus

An integrated IoT network optimizing energy consumption and waste management across the IZU campus.

eco About the Project

Smart Green Campus is a multidisciplinary initiative aiming to transform the IZU campus into a living lab for sustainability. By deploying a dense network of low-cost IoT sensors, we monitor real-time energy usage, water quality, and waste bin levels.

The data collected is aggregated into a central dashboard accessible to university administration and students, fostering awareness and enabling data-driven decisions to reduce our carbon footprint.
