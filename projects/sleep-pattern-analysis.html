Sleep Pattern Analysis AI

Machine learning algorithms to classify sleep stages and detect abnormalities using single-channel EEG data.

info About the Project

Sleep disorders are widespread but often undiagnosed due to the cost of full polysomnography. This project utilizes deep learning to analyze data from affordable, consumer-grade EEG headbands.

By accurately staging sleep (REM, Deep, Light) and detecting micro-wakefulness, our model can screen for issues like sleep apnea and insomnia at home.

The Problem

Clinical sleep studies are improved but inaccessible for nightly monitoring.

Our Solution

AI-driven analysis of single-channel EEG signals for at-home sleep profiling.
