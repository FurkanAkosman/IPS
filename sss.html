How can I become a member of IPS?

expand_more

Membership applications open at the beginning of each academic year through our website. You can join our community by filling out the application form and following the specified steps.

Membership Page →

Do you provide financial support for projects?

expand_more

Yes, we provide seed capital and resource support to selected projects. The amount and conditions of support vary depending on the nature and needs of the project.

What is IPS's mission?

expand_more

IPS aims to bring together students, academics, and professionals from various disciplines to promote collaborative projects and spread interdisciplinary thinking.

Learn More About Us →

Do I need to be from a specific department to participate in your projects?

expand_more

No, it is absolutely not required. IPS is open to students and academics from all disciplines. We believe that different perspectives promote creativity and innovation.

How inclusive is your community? Can anyone join?

expand_more

IPS embraces diversity and inclusion as core values. We strive to create an environment where everyone can feel a sense of belonging, freely share their ideas, and be respected.

How can I get information about your events?

expand_more

All our events are regularly announced on the 'Events' page of our website and on our social media accounts. You can also stay informed about the latest updates by subscribing to our email newsletter.
