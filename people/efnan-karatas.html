Efnan Karataş

Molecular Biology and Genetics • Bachelor • Istanbul Sabahattin Zaim University

Areas of Interest

Efnan’s interests focus on molecular genetics, neuroscience, and biotechnology. She is particularly engaged in understanding gene expression regulation and neurobiological processes that influence cognitive and behavioral functions. Her interdisciplinary approach integrates molecular biology techniques with computational tools to explore brain-related mechanisms.

IPS Contributions

As an active member of the Interdisciplinary Project Society (IPS), Efnan contributes to the organization and coordination of academic workshops, collaborative research initiatives, and science communication projects. She supports project documentation, event planning, and interdepartmental collaboration within the IPS community.

Future Aspirations

Efnan aims to pursue advanced studies in molecular neuroscience, focusing on neurodegenerative diseases and neural plasticity. Her long-term goal is to contribute to translational research that bridges molecular discoveries with therapeutic applications and to promote international collaboration in interdisciplinary scientific projects.
