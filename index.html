Focus Areas for 2025

Our strategic goals for the upcoming year are centered around key areas of technological and scientific advancement.

neurology

Computational Neuroscience

We aim to deepen our understanding of neural circuits and their relation to behavior through advanced computational models and data analysis techniques.

  • Developing brain-computer interfaces (BCIs).
  • Simulating neural networks to study cognitive processes.
  • Analyzing large-scale neuroscience datasets.
biotech

Biotechnology Innovations

Our focus is on pioneering novel biotech solutions, from synthetic biology to biomaterials, to address pressing challenges in healthcare and environmental sustainability.

  • Engineering smart biomaterials for tissue regeneration.
  • Exploring CRISPR-based gene editing technologies.
  • Designing biosensors for early disease detection.
psychology

AI in Cognitive Science

This area investigates the intersection of artificial intelligence and human cognition, using machine learning to model mental processes and enhance human capabilities.

  • Building AI models for language and learning.
  • Studying decision-making with reinforcement learning.
  • Investigating AI ethics and cognitive biases.
