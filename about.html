The Manifesto

Our Founding Statement

In an age of increasing specialization, the walls between disciplines have grown too high. We believe the most impactful questions cannot be answered by a single field of study.

We created IPS to dismantle academic silos and rebuild knowledge through collaboration, rigor, and experimentation.

Innovation happens at the intersections—where disciplines overlap and new methods emerge.

We commit to open inquiry, evidence-based debate, and reproducible outputs that can be shared with the community.

The IPS Founding Committee
